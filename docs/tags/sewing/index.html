DIY Ortlieb City bike pannier bag organiser

Alan Reed

17/03/2021
Official Ortlieb pannier bag organisers are expensive, so here I document my attempt to construct a homemade substitute. The finished product consists of a series of pouches sewn togther using heavy fabric, and is held inside the pannier using velcro.
